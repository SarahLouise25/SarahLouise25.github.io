About me

Heyo, I'm Daniel - a designer, developer, and behaviorial researcher hoping to further my career in the design space.

I was born and raised in Dallas, Texas. Growing up, I had a unique perspective of the world - being American, and being black. I learned everyone has a story to tell, and it's important to be open to change and fresh ideas.

I found a love for psychology and design my sophomore year of college. After personal struggles with anxiety and depression, I became fascinated with the way people think, feel, and behave - and how products could help them live more fulfilling lives. I graduated from the University of Virginia, with a B.A. in Computer Science and a Focus in Psychology.
